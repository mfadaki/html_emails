Events:

External Events:
To stay up-to-date with the contemporary research on supply chain and logistics management, a series of events are organised each year in which high-profile academics and/or academics who has published an interesting topic are invited from various universities across the world. The first event is organised to be held in November in which Professor Dmitry Ivanov will share his expertise on a very interesting topic: Ripple Effect, Resilience and Viability in Supply Chains. The invitation will be posted shortly.
Academic staff and HDR students are invited to the external events.
Internal Events:
Almost all leading business schools have internal research events in which the staff are presenting their current working paper or grant proposal to their colleagues. These events provide an opportunity (i) to boost the collaboration among the staff, and (ii) provide an opportunity for the presenter to receive constructive feedback from his/her colleagues before submitting the paper to a journal.
From November 2021, a series of working paper events are organised on a biweekly basis. Given that there are between 20-25 staff in our department, each staff will present for 30 minutes every year. Dates of presentation are ordered based on the academic staff name's alphabetical order.
